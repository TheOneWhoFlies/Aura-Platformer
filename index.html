AURA MONSTER

About & How to Play

Play Aura Platformer now and experience the fun playing as the Aura Monster himself, Natsuki Daniel on his journey to climb up the stairs! Climb up the stairs, defeat enemies, and show everyone your aura.

Controls:
Left Arrow - Move Left
Right Arrow - Move Right
Up Arrow - Jump

Aura Gimmick:
Every checkpoint you pass or enemy you beat without being hit increases your aura. This aura does damage over time to nearby enemies. The more aura in your aura meter, the stronger this effect is. At maximum aura you might be able to find some secret interactions.
